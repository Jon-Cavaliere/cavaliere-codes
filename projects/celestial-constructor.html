Celestial Constructor

This was a fun project that I completed. The goal was to create a piece of software that could be used for creative expression and interesting visuals. After playing around with it and seeing what was possible, I settled on the name Celestial Constructor because the majority of the systems that can be created look similar to galaxies. It was written in Java.
