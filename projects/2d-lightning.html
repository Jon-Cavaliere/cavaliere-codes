Procedural 2D Lightning

This was a quick Unity project I created as a supplment to a game that I'm working on. The idea was to come up with an algorithm that could mimic the basic shape of a bolt of lightning. I think the results are pretty cool.

Features

The design that I came up with allows the specification of any two points, and the amount of generation steps. The program will generate a bolt between those two points at the specified level of "Jaggedity"

My Reponsibilites

I was responsible for this entire project from planning to implementation.
